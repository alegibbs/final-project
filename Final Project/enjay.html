The Letter Enjay

In the Enjay project, I took on the challenge of creating a 27th letter for the alphabet, drawing inspiration from the well-known Impact typeface. Guided by my preference for chunky and straightforward fonts, I meticulously designed the letter "Enjay'' using Adobe Illustrator. The goal was to seamlessly integrate this new letter into the existing alphabet, ensuring it harmonized with the established characters while maintaining its unique identity in terms of shape and design. The project not only showcased my proficiency in Adobe Illustrator and the pen tool but also deepened my understanding of design principles, typeface anatomy, and the intricate balance required to introduce something new while fitting seamlessly into an existing framework. Through Enjay, I not only expanded my technical skills but also developed an appreciation for the art and precision of typography.
