GRAPHIC DESIGN PORTFOLIO

Welcome!

Hi, I am Lexi Gibbs, a passionate designer currently navigating the captivating landscape of Comprehensive Design. This interdisciplinary program has been a journey through the broad world of design possibilities. At its core, Comprehensive Design has introduced me to the power of design thinking—a problem-solving approach that delves into the heart of social challenges and user needs. In a world that often emphasizes specialization, this major stands out, encouraging a comprehensive understanding of the design process. It's more than just creating; it's about fostering connections and collaboration across diverse disciplines. Beyond the classroom, I bring my design passion to life as a graphic designer for student affairs marketing at Indiana University. Here, I apply the principles I've learned in Comprehensive Design to craft engaging visual narratives that resonate with our student community. This dual experience empowers me with a unique blend of skills—bridging the theoretical with the practical, and allowing me to contribute meaningfully to the dynamic world of design. It's not just a career; it's a human-centered adventure into the intersection of creativity and communication.
