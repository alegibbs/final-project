Find Your Fun

"Find Your Fun" (FYF) holds a special place as one of my initial projects with IU Student Affairs Marketing. This project centered around a recreational festival dedicated to promoting positivity and spreading information about health and overall well-being. The design for FYF was intentionally simplistic, aiming for a colorful and inviting aesthetic akin to popular trends in visual communication. The project involved the creation of diverse promotional materials, including eye-catching lawn signs, engaging digital ads, vibrant stickers, and merchandise like t-shirts and tote bags. Each element of the design was carefully crafted to resonate with the target audience, encapsulating the essence of the festival's cheerful and inclusive atmosphere.
