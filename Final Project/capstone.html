Nourish Network | An Indiana University Food Security Initiative

The app stands as a pivotal outcome of an intricate food service system design, strategically connecting surplus food from campus dining halls with students experiencing food insecurity. The development process involved extensive interviews with dining hall staff, students, and the food pantry, ensuring that all stakeholders’ perspectives were considered in crafting an effective and efficient solution. Through this comprehensive system design, the app not only streamlines the donation, management, and distribution processes but also actively engages the school in tackling food insecurity. By significantly enhancing the food pantry’s inventory, the app extends vital support to a larger number of students, emphasizing a commitment to community well-being. Furthermore, the app serves as a beacon of awareness, potentially reaching more students in need and informing them about available resources for assistance. In essence, this app, born from a meticulous system design, exemplifies a holistic approach that addresses both sustainability and the critical issue of food insecurity at IU, fostering a more collaborative and supportive campus environment.
