The Vault Pub

Designing for The Vault Pub, a recently opened restaurant at IU in the heart of campus, stands out as one of my favorite projects. Tasked with creating the food and drink menus, along with all additional ads and signage, I had the unique opportunity to contribute to the branding of a legitimate restaurant right on my own school grounds. This project spanned several months and presented a series of challenges, including tight deadlines and the need to produce multiple sizes and versions for menus, ads, and signage. Despite the fast-paced nature, the experience was thoroughly enjoyable as it allowed me to immerse myself in the creation of a brand for a local establishment, adding a personal touch to the campus dining experience. The project not only showcased my design skills but also taught me valuable lessons in managing time, meeting deadlines, and adapting to the diverse needs of a bustling restaurant environment.
